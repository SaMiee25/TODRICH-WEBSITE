About Todrich International

One family, three powerful ways to transform your future — Tech, Travel & Agriculture

Our Story

Founded by Uncle Todrich, our journey began with a simple belief: everyone deserves access to quality education, global opportunities, and healthy food. What started as a small tech training initiative has grown into a multifaceted group serving hundreds of students, travelers, and families across Nigeria and beyond.

We don't just teach code — we build careers. We don't just book tickets — we open doors. We don't just grow crops — we nourish communities.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with practical tech skills, connect them to global opportunities, and provide farm‑fresh produce — creating lasting impact in every community we serve.

Our Vision

To be Africa's most trusted ecosystem for tech education, international career placement, and sustainable agriculture — all under one roof.
